About

Sana is a locally trained language model built from scratch.

The 25M and 100M Sana models were trained under significant financial and hardware constraints. The 25M model was pretrained on approximately 2B tokens, while the 100M model was pretrained on approximately 4B tokens, with both models receiving only around 5,000 supervised fine-tuning samples. Because of this limited training scale, the models are not expected to match the fluency, factual accuracy, or reasoning ability of larger established models.

For comparison, GPT-2 124M was trained on WebText, a much larger corpus of roughly 40GB of text, commonly estimated at around 8–10B tokens. This is more than double in parameter count to the 100M Sana model, but it was trained on a larger and more established dataset, which helps explain its stronger fluency and text-generation quality.
